# Retail Sales Dashboard (2024–2025) 📊

## Project Overview
This project presents a retail sales dashboard built to analyze sales performance, product categories, and regional profits for the years 2024 and 2025.

The dashboard helps businesses understand sales trends, product performance, and regional profitability.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence

## Key Metrics
The dashboard highlights the following KPIs:

• Total Sales  
• Total Profit  
• Total Quantity Sold  

## Dashboard Insights

### 1️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend
A line chart compares monthly sales for 2024 and 2025 to identify seasonal patterns and growth trends.

### 2️⃣ Sales by Product Category
Technology products generated the highest revenue followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.

### 3️⃣ Profit by Region
Profit distribution across regions:
- West
- South
- East
- North

### 4️⃣ Filters
The dashboard allows users to filter data by:
- Year (2024 / 2025)
- Region (East, North, South, West)

## Key Business Insights
- Technology category contributes the highest sales (~4M).
- Sales growth is visible in October and November.
- West region generates the highest profit.
- Office supplies contribute minimal revenue compared to other categories.
